Financial Data Category Descriptions

Income Items

  • Maintenance Charges Common monthly fees paid by shareholders to cover building operations.
  • Parking Revenue generated from onsite parking space rentals.
  • Laundry Income from the building's laundry room facilities.
  • Transfer Fees Fees collected during the sale or transfer of apartment units (Flip Tax).
  • Interest Earnings from the building's bank accounts and investments.
  • Miscellaneous Occasional or non-recurring income from various small sources.
  • Special Assessment Temporary additional charges for specific large-scale projects or deficits.
  • Storage Revenue from renting out storage lockers or spaces to residents.

Expense Items

  • RE Taxes Real Estate property taxes paid to the municipality.
  • Utilities Costs for heating (oil/gas) and common area electricity.
  • Payroll Wages, benefits, and taxes for building staff (Super, porters, etc.).
  • Mortgage P&I Principal and Interest payments on the building's underlying mortgage.
  • Repairs General maintenance and upkeep of the building's systems and structure.
  • Insurance Premiums for building liability, property, and workers' compensation.
  • Security Costs for security personnel or automated monitoring systems.
  • Management Fee Fees paid to the external property management company.
  • Water & Sewer Municipal charges for water consumption and sewage services.
  • Legal & Audit Professional fees for legal counsel and annual financial audits.
  • Corp Tax & Misc Corporate filings, minor taxes, and administrative expenses.

Assets & Capital

  • Capital Expenditures Major investments in long-term assets (roofs, elevators, boiler replacements).
  • Cash and Cash Equivalents Liquid assets held in checking or savings accounts for immediate use.
  • Reserve Fund Dedicated funds set aside for future emergencies or major capital projects.
  • Total Liduidity Always add both Cash and Cash Equivalents and Reserve Fund together. Very important for accuracy.
  • Special Assessments These were in effect in 1993 & 2002 thru 2006 you may want to exclude for calculations.
  • Fire and Water Damage There was a one time fire and water damage charge in 2024 for $228,074 keep this in mind for your calculations.
