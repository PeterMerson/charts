Mortgage Principal Payments are included in all expense and net income figures on these charts.
These charts reflect the full cash cost of operating the building each year — including principal repayment on the underlying mortgage. Mortgage Principal is excluded from the audited Income Statement by accounting convention, but represents a real cash obligation paid by shareholders. The Riveredge Matrix allows toggling Principal on or off; these charts always show the complete cash picture.
